About AI Deck

Our Mission 🚀

At AI Deck, we're on a mission to revolutionize how people discover and use AI tools. We believe AI shouldn't be intimidating or hard to access — so we’re making it simple, centralized, and super user-friendly.

What We Offer 💡

AI Deck brings 40+ of the dopest AI tools under one digital roof for:

  • 🧠 Image & Video Generation
  • ✍️ Content Creation & Code Assistants
  • 🎤 Voice Cloning & Chatbots
  • 📄 Resume Builders & Productivity Tools
  • 🎨 AI Art & Animation Platforms
  • ... and a lot more coming soon!

Why We Built It 🛠️

Every Gen Z dev has been there — hopping from one AI site to another just to find *one good tool*. Tiring, right?
We created AI Deck to solve that hustle. It’s your one-stop AI playground, crafted with ❤️ using HTML, CSS, JS, and CSV (yes, we love keeping it lightweight and fast).

Our Vision 🌍

To empower students, creators, and professionals globally with seamless access to AI magic — from college project hacks to building businesses. Whether you're an artist, coder, YouTuber, or entrepreneur — AI Deck is your toolbox.

Core Values 💯

  • 🔓 Openness – AI for everyone, not just tech giants.
  • ⚡ Simplicity – Clean UI, fast access, no fluff.
  • 💬 Community – Built *by* students, *for* everyone.
  • 🌱 Growth – Constantly evolving with user feedback.

Tech Stack 🧩

  • 💻 HTML, CSS & JavaScript – For sleek and responsive frontend.
  • 📁 CSV – Lightweight data handling without needing a backend.
  • 🎨 Icons8 & Google Fonts – For those extra ✨ UI vibes.
